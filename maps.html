Maps

Every one of the 242,439 panoramas, pooled across all years (2013–2019) and split into twelve calendar-month maps, color-coded by data collection phase. Pooling by month rather than by year shows where the sampling grid was covered densely and where coverage thins out, independent of which year a given panorama happened to be captured.
